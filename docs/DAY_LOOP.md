# Day Loop — Wormwood

## Overview

Each in-game day = one session (~10 min). Days are numbered. The story advances on specific days regardless of player action (some events are fixed), but most outcomes depend on accumulated choices.

---

## Phase 1 — Morning: The Greenhouse

Screen: greenhouse interior, warm early light.

- Player tends plants (watering, pruning, harvesting)
- Limited actions — can't do everything, must prioritize
- Blight/pest events surfaced here
- Any harvested ingredients go directly to inventory
- Grandma's journal may have a morning note (early days especially)

**Ends when:** player clicks "Open Shop" or all actions used.

---

## Phase 2 — Day: The Shop

Screen: shop counter, street-facing window, shelves of jars.

Customers arrive one at a time (3–4 per day). Each interaction:

1. **Greeting** — customer describes why they've come, short dialogue
2. **Questions** — player chooses 1–2 follow-up questions (diagnosis)
3. **Symptom board** — player pins symptoms, selects diagnosis
4. **Mixing** — player opens remedy interface, combines ingredients
5. **Choice** — treat / refuse / overcharge / treat free
6. **Outcome** — reaction, coin exchange, relationship note

Between customers: small ambient moments (a sound from outside, a letter pushed under the door, a plant wilting visibly).

**Ends when:** last customer served or player closes shop early.

---

## Phase 3 — Evening: The Back Room

Screen: grandma's back room — desk, shelves, a locked door.

- Review inventory and coin
- Plant growth advances (visual — can see tomorrow's state)
- **Narrative beat**: a piece of something. A page from the journal. An object on the desk you didn't notice before. A memory Mara narrates.
- Optional: write a note (player journal — freeform text, saved)

**Ends when:** player clicks "Sleep" or interacts with all available objects.

---

## Phase 4 — Night: The Event

Screen: dark, minimal. Mara's perspective.

One event drawn from a weighted pool based on current day + story state:

- A knock at the door (answer or don't)
- A letter slid under
- A dream (abstract, narrative — foreshadowing)
- A sound from the locked greenhouse
- Silence (rarest — but Mara thinks, and the thought matters)

Events are short (30 seconds–1 minute of text/interaction). Some are purely atmospheric. Some advance the mystery. Some are traps.

**Ends when:** event resolves → next day begins.

---

## Day Progression Notes

- Days 1–3: tutorial framing, all phases guided
- Day 4 onward: player has agency over prioritization
- Some days have **forced events** (Brennan's first visit, Voss introduction, journal discovery) that override the night pool
- Resource pressure increases around day 7–10 (common ingredients run low if player hasn't planned)
- The locked greenhouse door can first be interacted with on day 6
