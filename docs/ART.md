# Art Direction — Wormwood

## Overall Feel

Painterly 2D. Warm but decaying. Like an old apothecary illustration left too long in a damp drawer — the colors have deepened, the edges softened, but the detail is still there.

Not pixel art. Not anime. Closer to: hand-painted backgrounds, illustrated characters, botanical-print aesthetic for plants and ingredients.

Reference mood: *Disco Elysium* (painterly, dense), *Spiritfarer* (warm 2D, expressive), old apothecary woodcuts and herbalist illustrations.

---

## Palette

**Shop / interior:** warm ambers, deep browns, dusty greens. Candlelight yellows. The feeling of late afternoon through a fogged window.

**Greenhouse:** brighter, more saturated greens. Humid. A contrast to the rest of the shop — this is where things are alive.

**Night scenes:** deep indigo, near-black. Minimal. A candle or street lamp as the only warm source.

**Regime / guard presence:** desaturated greys, institutional blues. Cold. Intrudes on the warmth.

---

## UI

- Parchment / aged paper texture for inventory, recipe book, symptom board
- Handwritten-style fonts for grandma's notes, journal entries
- Clean serif for UI labels — readable, slightly formal
- No bright UI chrome — everything feels like it belongs in the world

---

## Characters

Static illustrated portraits, expressive. Multiple expressions per character (neutral, concerned, suspicious, warm). No animation needed — emotional weight comes from writing + portrait changes.

Grandma Elsa: never shown directly. Referenced in objects, handwriting, how others describe her. The player builds her image. Consider showing only her hands in memory sequences.

---

## Plants

Botanical-illustration style. Each plant has a detailed "specimen" view used in the identification minigame. Consistent with old herbalist print books.

Key plants to design early:
- Wormwood (Artemisia absinthium) — silvery-green, feathery leaves
- Moonleaf (fictional) — pale, almost luminescent, used in the compound
- Thornroot (fictional) — dark, gnarly root, common remedy base
- Hearthbloom (fictional) — warm orange flower, calming properties
