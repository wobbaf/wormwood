# Wormwood

> *A bitter herb. Also: deep grief, bitterness — something that causes suffering.*

Wormwood is a narrative apothecary game set in a quietly oppressive city. You inherit your grandmother's shop after her sudden death. You tend her plants, serve her customers, mix her remedies — and slowly realize she didn't just die. She was silenced.

**Genre:** Narrative puzzle / shop sim  
**Platform:** PC (Steam)  
**Engine:** Godot 4  
**Session length:** ~10 minutes per in-game day
